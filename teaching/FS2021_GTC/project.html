CS 5408: Game Theory for Computing - Fall 2021

Textbook Information

This course has no textbook. However, we will follow a few reference books and some papers listed below:
  1. Algorithmic Game Theory, Edited by Noam Nisam, Tim Roughgarden, Eva Tardos and Vijay V. Vazirani
  2. Multiagent Systems by Yoav Shoham and Kevin Leyton-Brown
  3. Networks, Crowds and Markets by David Easley and Jon Kleinberg
