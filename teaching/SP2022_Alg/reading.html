Textbook Information

This course has no textbook. However, we will follow a few reference books and some papers listed below:

Books (DRM-Free Electronic Copies available through S&T Library)

  1. Data Structures and Algorithms with Python, by Kent D. Lee, Steve Hubbard
  2. Learning Algorithms, by George Heineman
  3. Hands-On Data Structures and Algorithms with Python, by Basant Agarwal and Benjamin Baka
  4. Python Algorithms: Mastering Basic Algorithms in the Python Language, Second Edition, by Magnus Lie Hetland
Students also have access to "Art of Computer Programming" written by Prof. Donald Knuth for free through campus library:
  1. Vol. 1: Fundamental Algorithms
  2. Vol. 2: Seminumerical Algorithms
  3. Vol. 3: Sorting and Searching
  4. Vol. 4A: Combinatorial Algorithms - Part 1
  5. Vol. 4 (Fasc. 1): Bitwise Tricks & Techniques; Binary Decision Diagrams
  6. Vol. 4 (Fasc. 6): Satisfiability
